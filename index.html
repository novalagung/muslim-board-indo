Muslim Board

Muslim Board is personal dashboard for muslim, available on all modern browsers for free via the official addon marketplace. This extension was inspired by Momentum, and currently supports the following languages:

  • English language (en)
  • Arabic (ar)
  • Bahasa Indonesia (id)
  • Traditional Chinese (zh-TW)

Prayer Times

Prayer times are displayed based on user's location.

Thanks to aladhan.com for providing the data.

Quotes

We display many good quotes and reminders. We are super selective on which type of content should be available on our extension. It should not contain anything related to spreading hatred toward others.

We ensure all of our content contains zero hate hate-speech or other type of content that might offend other religions or beliefs.

 

Muslim board is an open source projects. Meaning anyone allowed to contribute to us. We will talk in details about it on contribution section.

Images

We also surface high quality image as background to refresh our eyes.
Images are displayed randomly every few seconds.

TODO List

TODO List feature is super useful for keeping your important notes.

Contribution

We are a firm believer of Open Source!

Anyone is allowed to make a contribution to this project, it could be in form of adding new quotes or images.
For images we suggest hosting them on Unsplash, and we will just load it directly from there.

If you are a software engineer, simply submit a PR to our GitHub repo.
Other than, that please use my email and drop your contribution there.
Appreciate the help!
